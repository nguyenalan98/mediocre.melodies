Our Mission

The Mediocre Melodies is a revolutionary a cappella group that is truly the first of its kind. We are, quite frankly, average singers who have always wanted an a cappella experience but don't quite have what it takes to make one of the big ones! We are co-ed and focused on having fun and making friends while also creating some beautiful music along the way. Along with being an a cappella group, we donate all possible proceeds to charity.
